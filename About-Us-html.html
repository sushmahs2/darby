Our History

Welcome to the world of innovation and relentless pursuit of cutting-edge technology - welcome to the story of Darby Company.

Founded in 2010, Darby Company emerged from the visionary minds of a diverse group of individuals hailing from different cities across South India. United by a shared passion for exploration and a drive to introduce pioneering technologies to India, our journey began with a quest to bring forth revolutionary advancements from across the globe.

Setting our sights on Dubai, a global hub of innovation, we embarked on a mission to expand our horizons internationally. It was here that we delved into the realm of Vertical Wall Printing, laying the foundation for Darby's pioneering services. The inspiration we gathered from Dubai's thriving tech landscape propelled us back to our homeland, India, with a burning desire to import crucial machinery that was essential for various industries.

Our next stop was China, a haven for technological marvels. In our quest for innovation, we explored an array of cutting-edge machinery - from Wood CNC Routers to state-of-the-art Laser Machines, from versatile Agricultural equipment such as Rice planters and wood cutters to revolutionary Agricultural Drones. Diversifying further, we ventured into the realm of 3D Printing, AVT Bikes, T-shirt Printing technology, and the game-changing Vertical Wall Printers.

Not limiting ourselves to machines alone, we recognized the significance of providing quality raw materials at competitive rates. Thus, we supply premium materials like MDF, acrylic, Plywood, and a myriad of other raw materials, ensuring the best quality at cost-effective prices.

Darby Company stands as a beacon of innovation, a bridge between technology and progress. Our relentless pursuit of excellence and our commitment to importing essential machinery and materials continue to drive us forward. With a steadfast dedication to bringing the latest advancements to India, we strive to empower businesses and industries with the tools needed to thrive in an ever-evolving world.

Join us on this transformative journey as we revolutionize industries, elevate businesses, and shape the future with innovation at its core. Welcome to Darby Company - where technology meets possibility.
